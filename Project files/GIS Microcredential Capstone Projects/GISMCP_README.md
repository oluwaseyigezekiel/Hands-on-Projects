# GIS Microcredential Capstone Projects
This folder contains four projects I conducted on the Ayobo - Olorunda road, a 5.1km long and 20m wide road that
runs from Megida, (Lon. 3.2417◦, Lat. 6.6006◦) through Ishefun, Olorunda, (Long. 3.2045◦, Lat. 6.5752◦) Lagos, Nigeria. 

These projects are part of my microcredential in Geospatial Data Collection, Analysis & Interpretation using GIS Technology (GIS) 
at Seneca Polytechnic, Canada. They include:
- [Military Checkpoint Establishment](https://storymaps.arcgis.com/stories/b0346aa962304698942fbeba130981c8/print): Developing GIS solutions for the strategic placement of military checkpoints.
- [Field Maps App for Spatial Data Collection](https://storymaps.arcgis.com/stories/09b02bcf85214aa798eb35d9c9fd218b/print): Utilizing the ESRI Field Maps app to gather and manage spatial data efficiently.
- [Real Estate Management](storymaps.arcgis.com/stories/a479728bce4747bd88cd9787b7e0ec7b/print): Defining a 200-meter jurisdiction for individuals who searching for housing within a specific proximity.
- [Traffic Analysis and Safety](https://oluwaseyigezekiel.github.io/portfolio/storymaps/traffic_analysis.pdf): Analyzing traffic patterns to improve road safety and optimize traffic flow.
